Our Firm

At Murphy Mahon Keffler & Farrier, our clients get the best of both worlds. Our attorneys offer the high-quality legal expertise often only available at larger law firms coupled with the personal attention of a smaller firm.

  • Commercial Transactions and Business Litigation

    Murphy Mahon Keffler & Farrier is a full-service commercial law firm. We represent businesses throughout the State of Texas in a broad array of transactional and litigation matters. Our clients range from publicly traded corporations to sole proprietorships in diverse industries, including oil and gas business, financial institutions, real estate, manufacturing and technology.

  • A Group of Experienced and Energetic Attorneys

    Our attorneys offer a unique combination of energy and experience that helps us in our quest to provide unparalleled legal representation for our business clients, both small and large.

  • We Understand the Importance of Effective Representation

    At Murphy Mahon Keffler & Farrier, we pride ourselves on offering value-based services to our clients. We seek to achieve the business objectives of our client in an efficient, but effective manner.

  • A Reputation for Excellence and Client Commitment

    Since the firm was founded in 1992, we have built a strong reputation in the Fort Worth business community as a firm who provides effective representation in sophisticated business matters. We enjoy a strong rate of referrals — both from our existing clients and from other attorneys in the Fort Worth area. We have longstanding relationships with several clients that date back to the firm’s inception.

Oil and Gas Law

Our firm offers experienced representation in all aspects of oil and gas transactions and litigation. We represent publicly traded corporations, large and small independent producers, individuals and trusts in sophisticated oil and gas law transactions and disputes.

Our oil and gas transactional practice is led by Pat Murphy, one of the most experienced oil and gas attorneys in the Fort Worth area. Mr. Murphy is board certified in oil, gas and mineral law by the Texas Board of Legal Specialization and is a frequent speaker on oil and gas law topics at industry and legal seminars. Murphy Mahon Keffler & Farrier is a full-service law firm for the oil and gas industry. We represent our clients in numerous types of energy transactions, including:

  • The purchase and sale of oil and gas properties
  • Farm-out and exploration agreements
  • Operating Issues
  • Oil and gas title matters
  • Asset-based production loans
  • Oil and gas loan workouts and restructuring agreements

In addition to these oil and gas transactions, we also offer our oil and gas clients a complete range of business law services, from business formation advice through mergers and acquisitions to licensing and distribution agreements.

Experienced Representation in Oil and Gas Law Disputes and Litigation

Murphy Mahon Keffler & Farrier also handles disputes and litigation that arise during all phases of oil and gas exploration, development and production. We have substantial experience in lease termination cases, royalty disputes, mineral title disputes and operating agreement disputes.

Energy Lending

Our experience in representing clients in oil and gas transactions has allowed our firm to develop a specialized expertise in energy lending. We represent both financial institutions and borrowers in secured credit transactions relating to the energy industry.

We represent several financial institutions in asset-based production loans and oil and gas loan workouts and restructuring. For more information about the legal services we provide to banks and financial institutions, please view the Banking and Finance page of this site. Some of the financial institutions we represent in energy lending transactions are shown on our Representative Clients page.

We also represent borrowers in energy lending transactions, including operators and developers. For more information about the legal services we provide to businesses in the oil and gas industry, please view the Oil and Gas Law page of this site.

Banking and Finance

Our law firm has a strong and well-respected practice in banking and finance law. We represent several financial institutions in all phases of their commercial lending practices. We also represent our clients in litigation arising from lending transactions.

Banking and Finance Transactions

We represent banks and other financial institutions in all phases of commercial and real estate lending transactions, including negotiation, documentation, closing and administration of the lending transaction. We represent several outstanding financial institutions, some of whom are shown on our Representative Clients page.

We are also experienced in representation of financial institutions in the specialized area of energy lending. More information about our energy lending practice is available on the Energy Lending page of this site.

Our firm also has experience and expertise in representing borrowers in financial lending transactions.

Banking Litigation

Our representation of financial institutions extends to litigation matters. We have experience in representing financial institutions as plaintiffs and as defendants in lawsuits arising from commercial lending transactions.
